ABOUT

HELP! A group of mutated potatoes have sabotaged and taken over our spaceship. They've killed all our crewmates and turned them into zombies. They are now heading to Earth. THEY MUST BE STOPPED. You are the only survivor Sargent Amogus. You MUST defeat the Hivemind and take back your vessel. It is a rocky road with a lot of enemies to defeat; some are even your former crewmates now turned zombies. Do you have what it takes? Can you avenge your crewmates and save the Earth? Only one way to find out....

Outbreak:PTO is the work of four talented individuals for their CSS 385 Game Development Class at the University of Washington, Bothell. Our theme for this class was "Potatoes." So we came up with a game idea and ran with it. We wanted to have fun while creating this game, so we looked at other places for insight. Our game was inspired by Among Us, Halo, Starwars, and other bullet hell and roguelike games. We also added our own twist, and the result is this game.


Outbreak:PTO is a top-down 2D shooter game. The game takes place in outerspace inside a spaceship crawling with potato themed enemies. As the player, you are expected to explore the map, acquire weapon and health upgrades, defeat enemies, and challenge the Boss. Either you defeat the boss and win the game, or you die trying.
